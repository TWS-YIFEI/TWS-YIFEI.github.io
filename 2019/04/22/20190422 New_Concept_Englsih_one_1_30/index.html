Lesson 1 Excuse me

text

Excuse me! 常用于与陌生人搭话
Yes?
Is this your handbag?
Pardon?
Is this your handbag?
Yes,it is.
Thank you very much.

words and expressions

excuse 原谅
pardon 原谅，请再说一遍
handbag (女用)手提包

Lesson 11 Is this your shirt?

text

Whose shirt is that?
Is this your shirt,Dave?
No sir. It’s not my shirt.
This is my shirt.My shirt is blue.
Is this shirt Tim’s?
Perhaps it is,sir.
Tim’s shirt’s white.
Tim!
Yes,sir?
Is this your shirt?
Yes,sir.
Here you are.
Catch! 接住!
Thank you sir.

words and expressions

whose shirt is that?
whose 疑问代词,在本句作定语，修饰shirt。
Is this shirt Tim’s?
Tim’s Tim的所有格形式
Here you are. / Here it is.
are/is 应重读。

text

blouse 女衬衫
her hers的区别？
her 形容词性物主代词，hers 名词性物主代词
her形容词后面要加名词，hers本身就是名词
My hair is long,but hers is longer than mine.
人称代词主格 I he she it you
人称代词宾格 me him her it you
形容词性物主代词 my his her it your
名词性物主代词 mine his hers its yours

text

Are his shoes dirty or clean?
They’re not dirty.They’re clean.

words and expressions

我用am，你用are，is连着他她它，其他人称都用are
i am | you are | he is | she is | it is |
we are | they are | these are | those are |
am/is pass tense was
are pass tense were
tense:
　　一般现在时am/is/are
　　一般过去时was/were
　　一般将来时will be
will be 将要成为什么
will 将要做什么
will be doing sth 将来进行时，将来某个时刻正在做某事
will do sth 一般将来时，打算做什么
